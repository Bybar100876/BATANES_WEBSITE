Marlboro Hills Batanes


Stunning view.! That's the first thing in my head when we visited Marlboro Hills in Batanes. A bucket list to me and my wife that we accomplished. The Ivatans are so kind, humble and trustworthy. When you visit the Marlboro Hills, you should be around 7-9am so that you're not exposed to extreme heat of the sun. A must see view, no words can say the beauty of Batanes.
